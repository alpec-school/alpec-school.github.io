REGISTRATION IS OPEN!

<span style="color:blue"><b>Student Accommodation Grant</b></span>: <i>Master and Ph.D. students may be eligible for a grant covering the accommodation. 
Interested students are invited to fill the registration form and uploading their CV in the process.
After the notification, the applicants will confirm their registration and pay the registration fee.
</i> 

### IMPORTANT DATES

* Student Accommodation Grant Deadline: 15 May 2026
* Notification to Grant Applicants: 24 May 2026
* Registration Deadline: 30 June 2026

### REGISTRATION 

Registration fee: 50€
* The fee includes the coffee breaks for the days of the school.


The registration form is available here: [https://webapps.unitn.it/form/en/Web/Application/summerschool/ALPEC_2026](https://webapps.unitn.it/form/en/Web/Application/summerschool/ALPEC_2026)

After applying, you will receive the payment instructions by email.

<span style="color:red"><b>IMPORTANT</b></span>: If you are unsure whether you will be attending, please wait and complete the form only once you are certain, as completing the form triggers the issuance of a payment slip and an invoice which, once issued, cannot be easily cancelled.
